How To Play

Match all the cards to win! Choose a card, then choose another card. If the cards match, they will remain flipped & turn golden. If the cards do not match, they will flip back over. Try to remember the cards before they've flipped back over.

Moderate - Extreme difficulty are best played on desktop or landscape orientation.
