Memory Game

How To Play

Match all the cards to win!

Choose a card, then choose another card!

If the cards match, they will remain flipped & turn golden.

If the cards do not match, they will flip back over.

Try to remember the cards before they've flipped back over!

Try changing the Card Style for a greater challenge!
